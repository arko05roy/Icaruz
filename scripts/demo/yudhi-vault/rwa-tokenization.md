# RWA tokenization landscape

Real-world asset (RWA) tokenization brings off-chain credit, treasuries, and real-estate cashflows on-chain. Major players:

- **Ondo** — tokenized treasuries
- **Maple** — institutional credit
- **Centrifuge** — invoice and asset-backed pools

Yields land in 4-12% with credit risk attached. Most pools are KYC-gated unless the issuer offers a permissionless wrapper. Regulatory exposure varies by jurisdiction (see [[malaysian-regulatory-context]] for the Malaysian read).

For positioning vs other stablecoin strategies, see [[stablecoin-yield-overview]].
