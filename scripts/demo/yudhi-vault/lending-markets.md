# On-chain lending markets

Aave V3 and Compound V3 (Comet) are the two main lending markets.

**Aave V3**: eMode boosts capital efficiency for correlated assets (e.g. 95% LTV between stables).

**Compound V3 (Comet)**: single-base-asset model — USDC is the base, all other assets are collateral only.

Sustainable rates land at 3-6% on USDC. Risk profile: smart-contract risk plus oracle manipulation in volatile markets.

Sits at the conservative end of the [[stablecoin-yield-overview|stablecoin yield spectrum]].
