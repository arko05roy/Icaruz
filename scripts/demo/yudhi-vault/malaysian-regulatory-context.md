# Malaysian DeFi regulatory context

Malaysia's Securities Commission framework treats most DeFi activity as outside the Capital Markets and Services Act unless the protocol issues a security token. Stablecoin holdings and lending are not currently classified as securities.

[[rwa-tokenization|RWA tokens]] that wrap regulated instruments (treasuries, equity) WILL fall under SC purview. Buyer beware.

Bank Negara has signalled AML/KYC enforcement on on-ramps and off-ramps, not on the underlying contracts. So the contracts themselves remain accessible; the friction is at the fiat boundary.
